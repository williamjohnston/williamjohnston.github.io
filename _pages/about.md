Hello! My name is William Johnston. I'm a syntactician, a semanticist, and a fieldworker. 

My research focuses on the structure of the verbal and prepositional domains, and I work with speakers of [Hmong](/hmong "Hmong language") to describe and analyze their language. 

I currently hold a Fonds de recherche du Québec postdoctoral fellowship at the Université du Québec à Montréal. [Richard Compton](https://www.compton.uqam.ca/en/) is my supervisor. My PhD is from McGill University, where I was supervised by [Jessica Coon](https://jessica.lingspace.org/) and [Luis Alonso-Ovalle](https://people.linguistics.mcgill.ca/~luis.alonso-ovalle/about/).

Outside of linguistics, I love cooking, the Minnesota Vikings, and writing.
