On Friday, November 11, I gave a colloquium talk at the University of Minnesota. The talk, which is based on my in-progress dissertation research, was titled "The verb phrase in Hmong: Serialization and event structure". Thank you to the linguistics department at the U of M for inviting me, and thank you to everyone who came out (virtually or in-person) to hear the talk!
